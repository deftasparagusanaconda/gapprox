no... its bad. semantic clarity is better than syntactic uniformity. instead do it like this:

var1 = Variable('x')
var2 = Variable('y')
param1 = Parameter(3)
param1 = Parameter(2, name='p')
const1 = Constant('π', value=math.pi)
const2 = Constant('ᵉ', value=math.e)
